About LoanApp

LoanApp is a smart solution to quickly apply for small loans.

Our app uses a machine learning model to predict whether your loan will be approved or rejected based on several factors, including your income, loan amount, credit history, and more. Here's how it works:

  • Step 1: Fill out the loan application form with your details.
  • Step 2: Our app processes the data using One Hot Encoding and scales the features.
  • Step 3: We apply a trained machine learning model to predict the loan approval status.
  • Step 4: You will get an immediate response on whether your loan is Approved or Rejected.

We ensure fast and accurate predictions based on data-driven models.
